About Company

Lett Security is a premier security solutions provider, offering professional guarding services, alarm response, and comprehensive security training. Our expertise ensures the safety and security of businesses, individuals, and communities.

Vision

To be the leading security service provider in the industry, recognised for our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement in the security solutions.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide top-tier security services that ensure the safety and well-being of our clients through professionalism, innovation, and highly trained personnel. We strive to uphold integrity, reliability, and excellence in all aspects of our security.

Security Guarding Division

We provide highly trained security personnel to safeguard businesses, homes, and industrial sites. Our guards ensure 24/7 surveillance, access control, and risk assessment for enhanced safety.

Alarm Response (Reaction Officers)

Our rapid response team acts immediately to security alarms, ensuring swift intervention. Officers minimize response times with GPS tracking and communication technology.

Security Training Academy

We offer comprehensive training for security personnel, covering defensive tactics, surveillance, and legal responsibilities. Our academy combines theory and practice for real-world readiness.

Firearm Training and Competency

Our firearm training programs ensure safe handling, compliance, and tactical usage. Training includes live-fire exercises, simulated threats, and firearm maintenance.

Types of Firearms

Pistols: Compact, one-handed firearms designed for self-defense and close-range use.

Rifles: Fire a single bullet with high accuracy over long distances, featuring a rifled barrel.
